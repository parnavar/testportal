METHOD: GOD-POD PHOTOMETRIC
GOD-POD Principle: Glucose oxidase (GOD) oxidizes glucose to gluconic acid and hydrogen peroxide ($H_2O_2$). Peroxidase (POD) then catalyzes the reaction of $H_2O_2$ with 4-aminophenazone and phenol to form a pink-colored quinoneimine dye. Intensity of the color is directly proportional to glucose concentration.
